JCR Industrial Coatings Inc. is a full-service industrial painting and protective coatings contractor headquartered in the Midwest. Since 1997, we have delivered high-performance coating systems for water storage tanks, elevated towers, bridges, and municipal infrastructure.

We combine deep technical expertise with a safety-first culture to protect the assets that communities depend on. Every project is executed to SSPC/NACE standards with full documentation and quality assurance.
