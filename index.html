Who We Are

Specialty Subcontractor.
Municipal Infrastructure.

JCR Industrial Coatings is a specialty industrial coatings subcontractor serving municipal water and wastewater infrastructure markets throughout Ohio and surrounding states. We deliver full-scope rehabilitation of elevated and ground storage tanks ranging from 100,000 to 2,000,000 gallons.

We partner with general contractors on public infrastructure projects requiring engineered specification adherence, inspection coordination, and structured documentation. Every scope we execute is backed by AMPP/NACE certified personnel, SSPC surface preparation standards, and a safety program built for work at elevation.
