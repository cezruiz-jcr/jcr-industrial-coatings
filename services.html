What We Do

Core Services

Full-scope industrial coatings execution for municipal water infrastructure, industrial facilities, and commercial properties.

Abrasive Blasting

SSPC SP-6, SP-10, and SP-5 surface preparation using recyclable steel grit and garnet media. Full containment with negative air pressure systems for zero-emission operations.

Full Containment Systems

Class 1A, 2A, and 3 containment systems designed for elevated and ground storage tanks. Engineered rigging, suspended scaffolding, and environmental compliance documentation.

Lead Abatement

EPA RRP certified lead paint removal with full worker protection protocols. Air monitoring, soil sampling, and proper disposal documentation per 40 CFR Part 745.

Structural Steel Repair

Weld repair, plate replacement, and structural reinforcement for deteriorated tank components. AWS D1.1 certified welders with PE-stamped repair drawings when required.

Protective Coating Application

High-performance epoxy, polyurethane, and fluoropolymer coating systems applied per manufacturer specifications. DFT verification, holiday testing, and full QA/QC documentation.

Interior Tank Lining

NSF/ANSI 61 certified potable water lining systems. Confined space entry protocols, ventilation engineering, and post-application disinfection coordination.

Water Tower Restoration & Maintenance

Full-cycle restoration and scheduled maintenance programs for public and private water towers. Inspection reports, condition assessments, and long-term maintenance planning to extend asset life and ensure AWWA compliance.

Consulting Services

Independent project consulting for municipalities, engineering firms, and general contractors. Scope development, specification review, contractor qualification, and quality assurance oversight for coating and rehabilitation projects.

Expanding Our Capabilities

Epoxy Flooring New Service

Industrial & Commercial Epoxy Floor Coatings

JCR Industrial Coatings is expanding its protective coatings expertise into industrial and commercial epoxy flooring. Leveraging the same high-performance coating knowledge and surface preparation standards we apply to water infrastructure, we deliver durable, seamless epoxy floor systems for warehouses, manufacturing facilities, garages, and commercial spaces.

Our epoxy flooring systems provide outstanding chemical resistance, impact durability, and a clean professional finish that is easy to maintain. Whether you need a standard solid-color system, a decorative flake floor, or a heavy-duty industrial coating, our team brings the same SSPC-trained surface prep and quality assurance to every floor project.

  • Surface preparation — shot blasting, grinding, and profile assessment
  • Moisture vapor barrier and primer systems
  • Solid color, decorative flake, and quartz broadcast systems
  • Chemical-resistant and anti-slip topcoat options
  • Warehouse floor striping and line marking
