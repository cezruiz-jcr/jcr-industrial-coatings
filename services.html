What We Do

Core Services

Full-scope industrial coatings execution for municipal water and wastewater infrastructure. Every service is delivered to SSPC surface preparation standards with AMPP/NACE certified oversight.

Abrasive Blasting

Dry and wet abrasive blasting executed to SSPC-SP surface preparation standards (SP-6, SP-10, SP-5). Proper anchor profile for coating adhesion and long-term performance.

Full Containment Systems

Engineered containment structures for elevated and ground storage tanks. Designed to capture blast media, prevent environmental release, and comply with lead abatement and EPA requirements.

Lead Abatement

Hazardous material handling and lead paint removal on aging municipal infrastructure. Executed in compliance with EPA RRP rules, OSHA 1926.62, and state-specific lead abatement regulations.

Structural Steel Repair

Weld repairs, joint sealing, and steel rehabilitation executed under current OSHA and AWS standards. Sikaflex joint sealing and holiday testing included in all applicable scopes.

Coating System Application

Application of high-performance epoxy, urethane, and plural component coating systems specified for potable water contact and industrial environments. Interior and exterior application capability.

Interior Wet & Dry Lining

Interior tank lining for potable water storage — wet and dry abrasive blasting, NSF 61-compliant coating systems, holiday testing, and final disinfection per AWWA C652 standards.
